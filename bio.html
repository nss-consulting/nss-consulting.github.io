How I've helped clients lighten the load

Is your team spread too thin?

Clients often bring me in to help with projects when their full-time staff are spread too thin. Thanks to my years of experience, I can immediately lead project tasks on my own or contribute to team deliverables. Whether you need study design, analysis, project management, reporting, or all these things — I’m able to hit the ground running and provide the quality that your end client expects.

Most of my regular clients are small and mid-sized consulting firms, and one recently brought me onto a quick-turn project that coincided with other project activities and staff vacation schedules. I developed the discussion guide, managed participant recruitment, conducted thematic analyses, and led reporting so that the firm could meet their client’s timeline to their usual high level of quality. Thanks to our collaboration on this project, the firm was able to manage their team’s energies sustainably while they were otherwise short-staffed. Furthermore, the successful delivery of this project guaranteed follow-on work for their business.

Many organizations I work with see me as a trusted member of the team.

I step into your organization as needed and provide you with a way to scale without burning out your full-time staff. Whether your teams are taking on additional work, temporarily losing team members to medical/family leave, or filling gaps while you seek additional full-time employees, I help your business continue thriving through its ebbs and flows.

Let’s talk about how I can lighten the load for your team!
