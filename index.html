Best Practices for Survey Design

Designing Effective Surveys

I always say that designing an effective survey is a bit of art and science. At the end of the day, I want to design a questionnaire that respondents are inclined to complete, that gets my clients the information they want to learn, that reduces respondent bias, and that is easy to analyze. To craft a questionnaire that meets these objectives, I have some basic design principles that I apply to my work:

  • Be clear on what you want to learn
  • Know your audience
  • Maximize responses
  • Utilize techniques that reduce respondent bias
