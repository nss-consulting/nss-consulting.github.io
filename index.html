Nicole Steward-Streng

I bring more than a decade of experience in social science research and analytics into my data storytelling to help clients make data-informed decisions. have extensive experience in primary data collection, quantitative and qualitative methodologies, and complex analysis. I am passionate about understanding the human experience and have spent my career using data to determine how to make the human journey a little easier. I am a certified Project Management Professional (PMP) with experience managing numerous research projects. My experiences in academic research, as well as both client- and supplier-side marketing research, have uniquely positioned me to have a multifaceted toolbox of analytical skills, leadership, strategy development, and project management.
