When to Consider Qual

  • To gain a deeper understanding of individuals behaviors, attitudes, and needs
  • For topics where you want to be able to ask follow-up questions or probe for additional information
  • To understand how decisions are made/li>
  • In cases where it may be important to understand respondent tone or body language
  • For topics where it might be important to first establish rapport with participants
  • To observe how individuals interact with products or concepts

Methods I've Used

  • Individual Interviews (structures, semi-structured, unstructured)
  • In-Person Focus Groups
  • Virtual Focus Groups
  • Bullitan Board Focus Groups
  • Dairy Studies
  • Mobile Ethnographies
  • Shop-Alongs
  • Qualitative Usability Testing
