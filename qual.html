When to Consider Qualitative Methodologies

  • For topics where you want to be able to ask follow-up questions or probe for additional information
  • For topics where it might be important to establish rapport with participants
  • In cases where it may be important to understand respondent tone or body language
  • To gain a deeper understanding of individuals behaviors, attitudes, and needs
  • To understand individuals make decisions
  • To uncover unmet needs and barriers to behaviors
  • To observe how individuals interact with products or concepts

Primary Data Collection for Needs Assessment

End Client: Public School District

My Client: Consulting Firm

My Role: Lead Analyst

The consulting firm was tasked with conducting an inclusive needs assessment to inform the school district’s program and service planning. The district wanted to understand how students, parents, and faculty were experiencing the mental health services available within the district and to identify areas where the district might be able to step in to further meet their goals of accessible and equitable care.

I collaborated with the consulting project team to develop a series of interview and discussion guides to gather perspectives from students, parents/guardians, mental healthcare providers, school staff, faculty, and administrators (all separately). I moderated 90-minute focus groups with each of the following audiences: 1) the district’s parents and guardians, 2) mental healthcare providers; and 3) school staff and faculty . Additionally, I conducted 45-minute interviews with school administrators across the district . The purpose of these discussions was to illuminate gaps in support, identify strengths in current programs and services, and provide these stakeholders with a platform to share perspectives on possible solutions and priorities in improving the students’ behavioral health outcomes.

In addition to this data-collection , I was also responsible for developing the analytic strategy and leading the data analysis. I developed the initial draft of the codebook, and I worked with another analyst to refine the coding strategy while we coded the transcripts for interviews and focus groups using NVivo software. I also conducted thematic analyses for the parent focus groups, the faculty/staff focus groups, and the administrator interviews .

To conclude the project, I developed a topline report for the consulting team that identified key findings and recommendations for the end client, and my client incorporated these results and insights into the presentation slides and final report that they delivered to the school district.
