Delivering on Client Expectations

End Client: County Health Agency

My Client: Consulting Firm

My Role: Co-Project Manager

I came on as a Senior Consultant to assist a consulting firm in the second phase of a multi-phase project. Just weeks after I joined the team, the Project Manager announced her departure from the organization and entrusted me with the completion of the project’s second phase: finishing the data collection and analysis, as well as developing the final reports .

In this role, I was responsible for keeping the project on task and making sure that all deliverables were completed for the County Health Agency and other stakeholders on time. In monthly status updates that I provided for the County Health Agency, I described the project’s current and scheduled research activities. In addition to analyzing the qualitative interviews that took place among consumers and providers, I wrote the reports that identified the key themes and recommendations that emerged from these interviews, oversaw the development and timely delivery of a sustainability plan, and helped ensure that the recommendations were incorporated into the final project deliverables.

When the firm’s project manager stepped down mid-project, her departure was a major loss to the firm! But I supported the team through this transition, helping them complete the project successfully without delays, and the County Health Agency gave my client very positive feedback.
