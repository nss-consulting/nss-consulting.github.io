Delivering on Client Expectations

End Client: County Health Agency

My Client: Consulting Firm

My Role: Co-Project Manager

I came onto a project with a consulting firm to assist in the second phase of a multi-phase project as a Senior Consultant. Weeks into me joining the team, the Project Manager announced her departure from the organization and entrusted me to complete the second phase of the project, finishing data collection and analysis as well as developing final reporting.

I was responsible for keeping the project on task and making sure that all deliverables were provided to the County Health Agency and other stakeholders on time. I provided monthly status updates for the County Health Agency that described the current and scheduled research activities. In addition to the analysis and report writing for the qualitative research interviews that were conducted among consumers and providers, I also helped ensure that the sustainability plan was developed by another member of the project team and that the recommendations were incorporated into the final project deliverables.

Despite a major loss to the project team, the work was successfully completed with no delays and positive feedback from the end-client.
