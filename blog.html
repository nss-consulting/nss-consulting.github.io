How I've helped clientslighten the load

Is your team spread too thin?

Often my clients bring me in to help with projects when their full-time staff are spread too thin. With my years of experience, I am often able to jump right in to either lead project tasks on my own or contribute to team deliverables. Whether it’s study design, analysis, project management, or reporting, I’m able to hit the ground running and provide the quality that your end client expects.
I regularly contribute to projects for small and mid-sized consulting firms. Recently I was brought onto a project when a client of one of these firms requested quick-turn qualitative consumer interviews that coincided with several other active projects that the firm was completing. I was able to create the appropriate research protocols, manage participant recruitment, conduct the analyses, and lead the development of the final report.
For many of the organizations that I have worked with, I have become a trusted member of team.
Whether teams are taking on additional work, having members go out on medical/parent leave, or filling gaps while they hire additional staff, I can lighten the load in the ebs and flows of your business. I step into projects as needed and provide organizations with a way to make sure you can scale without burning out their full-time staff.
